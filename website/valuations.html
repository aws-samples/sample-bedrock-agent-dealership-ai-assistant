Part Exchange: The Smart Way to Upgrade

Part exchanging your current vehicle when purchasing a new one offers several key benefits:

  • Reduced paperwork - We handle all the vehicle ownership transfer paperwork
  • Tax advantages - Only pay VAT on the difference between your part-exchange and new vehicle
  • Convenience - Drive in your old car and drive away in your new one in a single visit
  • No waiting for payment - The value is immediately deducted from the price of your new vehicle
  • Potential for negotiation - We may be able to offer better deals on part-exchanges

Our team will guide you through every step of the process, ensuring a seamless transition to your new vehicle.

Getting the Best Value: Trade-in Tips

Gather Documentation

Bring your V5C logbook, service history, MOT certificates, and any warranty documentation to demonstrate your vehicle's care history.

Clean Your Vehicle

A clean car makes a better impression. Consider a professional valet service before your valuation appointment.

Minor Repairs

Fix small issues like bulbs, wiper blades or minor cosmetic damage, but avoid spending on major repairs before trading in.

Bring All Keys

Having all original keys and remotes can significantly impact your vehicle's value.

Include Accessories

Don't forget to bring any accessories that came with the car, such as roof racks, spare wheels, or cargo covers.

Timing Matters

Consider market trends when selling. Convertibles often fetch better prices in spring, while 4x4s may be more valuable in winter.

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to sell my car?

You'll need your V5C logbook, service history, MOT certificate, and all sets of keys. If your vehicle is still under finance, you'll also need your finance settlement letter.

Can I trade in a car that's on finance?

Yes, we can handle vehicles with outstanding finance. We'll work with your finance company to settle the remaining balance as part of the transaction process.

How long is the valuation valid for?

Our valuations are typically valid for 7 days, subject to inspection and verification of the vehicle's condition and history.

Do I need to have my car serviced before valuation?

While not required, a recent service can positively impact your valuation. However, we don't recommend investing in a major service immediately before trading in.

What if my car has damage or mechanical issues?

We accept vehicles in all conditions. While damage may affect the valuation, we're still interested in purchasing your vehicle. Be upfront about any issues to ensure an accurate valuation.

Can I trade in multiple vehicles?

Yes, we accept multiple vehicle trade-ins against a single purchase. Each vehicle will be valued individually.

Do I need to clean my car before the valuation?

While not mandatory, a clean car makes a better impression. We recommend at least a basic clean inside and out before your appointment.

How quickly can I get paid?

We can process payment on the same day once all paperwork is completed. Bank transfers typically clear within 24 hours.
